Rocky 4

Rocky IV is a high-stakes sports drama where Rocky Balboa faces his toughest opponent yet—Soviet powerhouse Ivan Drago. After Drago delivers a fatal blow to Rocky’s friend Apollo Creed, Rocky heads to Russia for an intense training showdown. Fueled by revenge and determination, he steps into the ring for a battle that’s more than just a fight—it’s a clash of pride, perseverance, and heart. With iconic montages and an unforgettable finale, Rocky IV remains one of the most electrifying entries in the franchise
