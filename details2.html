Çukur

Çukur is a Turkish crime drama that revolves around the powerful Koçovalı family, who rule the Çukur neighborhood in Istanbul. When their youngest son, Yamaç, returns home after years away, he is forced to take charge and protect his family from dangerous rivals. Filled with intense action, deep family bonds, and shocking betrayals, Çukur blends crime, drama, and romance in a gripping story of loyalty, power, and survival. Its unforgettable characters and powerful storytelling have made it one of Turkey’s most popular series.
