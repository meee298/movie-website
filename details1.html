The Blacklist

The Blacklist is a thrilling crime drama series that follows Raymond "Red" Reddington, a former criminal mastermind who mysteriously surrenders to the FBI. He offers to help capture the world's most dangerous criminals—but only if he works exclusively with rookie profiler Elizabeth Keen. As they unravel high-stakes cases, dark secrets about Red’s true motives and Liz’s past emerge. With intense twists, espionage, and unpredictable alliances, The Blacklist keeps viewers hooked through its gripping storytelling and brilliant performances.
