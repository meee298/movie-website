The Penthouse

The Penthouse is a gripping South Korean drama that revolves around the luxurious yet cutthroat world of wealthy families living in Hera Palace. Filled with intense rivalries, secrets, betrayals, and revenge, the series follows the power struggles of ambitious residents, particularly the ruthless Cheon Seo-jin, the vengeful Oh Yoon-hee, and the calculating Shim Su-ryeon. As their lives intertwine with lies and dark secrets, the show delivers shocking twists, emotional turmoil, and high-stakes drama, making it a must-watch for fans of suspenseful and dramatic storytelling.
